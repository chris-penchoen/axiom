# License Status

No public software or content license is granted by this Canon.

The Axiom Canon is shared for discussion and evaluation only. All rights are
reserved by the author pending a deliberate licensing decision.

A future public release may adopt a standard open license — likely a
documentation/content license (for example Creative Commons) for the theory prose
and a separate software license for any tooling — after:

- private and personal content is fully separated;
- repository history is audited;
- third-party material is reviewed;
- and the stable Canon boundary (per `RECONCILIATION.md`) is established.

Until then, treat this material as read-only reference: cite it, discuss it, and
evaluate it, but do not assume rights to redistribute or reuse it.

This posture mirrors the AxiomCE preview package's `LICENSE.md` so the theory and
its engine preview grant rights on the same, deliberate schedule.
